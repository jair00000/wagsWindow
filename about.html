Our Story

Hi, I'm Justine Wagner, founder of WAGS® Window Solution and a nationally recognized Madico dealer and certified subcontractor. I specialize in professional window film installation for both residential and commercial spaces, delivering custom solutions that enhance energy efficiency, comfort, privacy, and protection.

With years of hands-on experience, I've built WAGS® on a foundation of precision, integrity, and client-focused service. Whether it's solar control, decorative designs, or safety and security films, I take pride in offering top-quality work that meets the highest standards. Every project is a reflection of my commitment to reliable results and clean, professional installations.

I also partner with contractors, builders, and project managers to provide dependable subcontracting services, ensuring every film is installed with accuracy and care—on time and within scope. When you work with WAGS®, you're getting more than a product—you're getting a solution tailored to your needs.
