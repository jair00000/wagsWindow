What is Commercial Decorative Film?

Commercial Decorative Film is a specialty window film designed to enhance the appearance, privacy, and functionality of glass in business environments. Applied to interior or exterior glass surfaces, it features frosted, textured, or patterned designs that simulate etched glass—ideal for offices, lobbies, conference rooms, and storefronts. Decorative films allow natural light to pass through while providing visual separation, branding opportunities, and a modern, professional look—without the high cost of custom glass.
