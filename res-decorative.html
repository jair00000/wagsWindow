What is Residential Decorative Film?

Residential Decorative Window Film is a stylish and functional glass enhancement solution designed to add privacy, elegance, and personality to home interiors. Applied to existing windows, doors, or glass partitions, it offers frosted, textured, or patterned finishes that mimic etched glass—without the high cost. Ideal for bathrooms, entryways, and living areas, decorative films enhance privacy while allowing natural light to flow, blending beauty and practicality for a more refined home environment.
