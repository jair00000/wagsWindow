What is Residential Film?

Residential window film is a specialized material applied to home windows to improve comfort, privacy, and energy efficiency. It helps block heat, reduce glare, and protect interiors from UV damage, all while maintaining natural light and enhancing your home's appearance.

Key Benefits of WAGS® Residential Window Film

  • Energy Efficiency
    Lower household energy bills by reflecting and absorbing solar heat, reducing reliance on air conditioning and maintaining a cooler, more consistent indoor environment year-round.
  • Glare Reduction
    Enjoy clearer views of screens and a more comfortable home atmosphere by reducing harsh glare—perfect for living rooms, kitchens, and home offices.
  • UV Protection
    Block up to 99% of harmful UV rays to help preserve the color and lifespan of your furniture, flooring, artwork, and interior finishes.
  • Enhanced Comfort
    Create a balanced indoor climate by minimizing hot spots and improving temperature control throughout your home for year-round comfort.
  • Privacy & Style
    Choose from frosted, tinted, or decorative film options to increase privacy, elevate interior design, and complement your home's aesthetic.
  • Optional Security
    For added protection, certain films can be upgraded to improve glass strength and safety, helping protect your home from forced entry or impact.
