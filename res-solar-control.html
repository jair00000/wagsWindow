What is Residential Solar Control Film?

Residential Solar Control Film is a high-performance window film designed to reduce solar heat, block harmful UV rays, and minimize glare in homes. Applied directly to existing windows, it improves indoor comfort, lowers cooling costs, and helps protect furniture and flooring from sun damage—all while preserving natural light and your home's aesthetic appeal.
