What is Safety & Security Film?

Safety & Security Window Film is a durable, multi-layered glass protection solution designed to strengthen commercial windows against break-ins, accidents, and natural impacts. Professionally installed on existing glass, it holds shattered pieces together upon impact, reducing the risk of injury and deterring potential intruders. Ideal for offices, storefronts, schools, and public buildings, this film enhances safety, protects assets, and provides added security without compromising natural light or building aesthetics.

Key Benefits of WAGS® Safety & Security Films

  • Impact Resistance
    Strengthen glass surfaces to withstand break-ins, forced entry attempts, and accidental impacts, providing an essential layer of security.
  • Shatter Protection
    Hold shattered glass in place upon impact, reducing the risk of injury from flying glass and minimizing potential damage.
  • Intrusion Delay
    Deter intruders by increasing the time and effort needed to break through windows, giving security teams or law enforcement time to respond.
  • Enhanced Safety
    Protect building occupants from glass-related accidents during natural disasters, explosions, or workplace incidents.
  • UV Protection
    Block up to 99% of harmful UV rays, helping safeguard interiors, equipment, and merchandise from fading and UV damage.
  • Maintained Visibility
    Reinforce windows without compromising natural daylight, outdoor visibility, or the architectural look of your building.
  • Fast, Non-Disruptive Installation
    Professionally applied over existing glass with minimal business downtime and no major structural changes.
