What is Commercial Film?

Commercial window film is a multi-layered polyester coating applied to the interior or exterior of business windows to reduce heat, glare, and harmful UV rays while maintaining clear visibility. Designed for offices, retail stores, and other commercial buildings, it enhances energy efficiency, occupant comfort, and protects interiors from fading. By lowering cooling costs and improving the appearance of the building, commercial window tinting offers a smart, long-term solution that supports both performance and aesthetics in professional environments.
